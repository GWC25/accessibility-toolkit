Privacy & Your Data

Your privacy matters. Here's what you need to know:

  • What data is stored: Your saved tools, progress status, and settings preferences.
  • Where it's stored: Locally in your browser's storage on this device only.
  • Who can access it: Only you, on this device. No one else can see your data.
  • No tracking: We don't collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on servers.
  • Your control: You can export or delete your data at any time using the buttons above.
